Nam Khao Recipe

Description

Nam Khao is a Laotian dish that combines fresh herbs, crispy fried rice, and a tangy fish sauce dressing.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Red Curry Paste
  • Shallots
  • Fresh Herbs (cilantro, mint, thai basil)
  • Lime
  • Garlic
  • Green Leaf Lettuce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • A Neutral Oil

  1. Cook jasmine rice and let cool to room temperature.
  2. In a large bowl, combine rice, eggs, shallots, and curry paste.
  3. Form rice into patties and deep fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Make dressing to your liking with fish sauce, lime, garlic, and sugar.
  5. Crush or chop rice patties into nuggets. Toss with dressing and herbs.
  6. Serve with lettuce to make lettuce wraps.
